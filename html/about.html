WHO WE ARE

K.DAE is an independent fashion brand, born from whispered dreams and the desire for a world where everyone is free to express themselves. We believe that everyone has their own story - and clothing is a part of the journey to tell that story. At K.DAE, beauty doesn't come from chasing trends, but from the courage to live true to yourself.
"Timeless fashion. Designed for those who define their own style."
That is the spirit woven into every design we create - not only stylish, but also full of soul.

OUR STORY

The name K.DAE carries a special message. “K” stands for Karlis - the founder of the brand, the one who began the journey of making fashion a part of personal freedom. “DAE” is inspired by the Korean word( 대 ) , which means “goodness” or “greatness” - symbolizing the positive values K.DAE aims to share with the community.
Together, K.DAE is more than just a name - it's a statement: everyone deserves to live in their own way - to pursue what is beautiful, elegant, and sincere. We don't create fashion just to wear, but to inspire those who dare to break the mold.

MATERIALS & VALUES

At K.DAE, fabric is more than just material - it is where emotions begin. We believe that when a piece of clothing is made from kind and thoughtful materials, it brings a sense of comfort, lightness, and authenticity - just like your true self. That's why K.DAE prioritizes environmentally friendly fabrics such as cotton, linen, and viscose - materials that are soft, breathable, and sustainably enduring over time.
We do not follow fast fashion. Every design is crafted with care, down to each stitch - no rush, no mass production - because to us, true value lies in the dedication and emotion poured into every small detail.
We believe in sustainable fashion - not only in the materials we choose, but also in a mindful approach to consumption, in selecting pieces that can accompany you through many moments in life. A beautiful dress doesn't need to be extravagant. It just needs to make you feel like yourself - and that is true beauty.

OUR BELIEFS

At K.DAE, we believe that fashion is more than appearance - it is a silent language to tell your own story. Every design is built upon three core values: authenticity, sophistication, and freedom of expression. We embrace genuine emotions and individuality, celebrate elegance in every detail, and always encourage everyone to be themselves - free from rules or standards.
K.DAE envisions a world where fashion knows no boundaries of gender, body shape, or prejudice. We believe that beauty does not come from perfection, but from the courage to live true to who you really are. That is why every K.DAE piece carries a message: wear what makes you feel comfortable, free, and inspired - with confidence.
